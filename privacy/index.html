Privacy Policy

Data not collected.

Last updated: April 26, 2026

The short version

Inventist LLC ("Inventist," "we," "us") builds native iOS apps. Our apps — including Whereabouts — are designed to work without an account, without tracking, and without sending your data anywhere. Below is the App Store nutrition label for everything we publish.

CategoryWhat we collect
Data Used to Track YouNone
Data Linked to YouNone
Data Not Linked to YouNone
Third-party SDKsNone
AnalyticsNone
AdvertisingNone

What stays on your device

Our apps store the things they need to work — your progress, streaks, settings, and unlocked content — locally on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults and the local file system. None of it leaves your phone. If you uninstall the app, that data is gone with it.

Purchases

If you choose to subscribe or buy a one-time unlock, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We don't see your name, your card, or your Apple ID — we only receive a receipt token from Apple confirming that the purchase is valid. That token lives on your device.

Third parties

We don't integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. Our apps make no network requests for the purpose of identifying you.

Children

Our apps are designed to be safe for all ages, including children. Because we don't collect personal information from anyone, we don't collect personal information from children either. Whereabouts is rated 4+ on the App Store.

Your rights

Since we don't store your data on our servers, there's nothing for us to look up, export, or delete on your behalf. You can wipe everything our apps know about you instantly by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy — for example, if a future app needs an account — we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above before the change takes effect, and we'll surface the change inside the app itself.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to contact@inventist.io. Mail can be addressed to Inventist LLC, Apopka, Florida, USA.
