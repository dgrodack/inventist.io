Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026 · Applies to inventist.io and all Inventist apps.

Inventist LLC ("Inventist", "we", "our") makes native iOS apps. This policy explains what we do — and, mostly, do not do — with your data.

The short version

We do not collect, sell, or share personal data through our apps. We do not use third-party trackers or advertising SDKs. Game progress is stored on your device. That's it.

What our apps store

Our apps (including Whereabouts) store the following locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs (UserDefaults and the StoreKit framework):

  • Your in-app preferences (haptics on/off, units of measure, etc.)
  • Game state: streaks, scores, XP, badges, current puzzle progress
  • Records of in-app purchases you've made (managed by Apple's StoreKit)

None of this is transmitted to Inventist or any third party. You can erase all of it at any time by deleting the app.

If you have opted in to Share Analytics with App Developers in iOS Settings, Apple may share anonymized crash and performance reports with us. This is controlled by you in Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements.

Purchases and subscriptions

In-app purchases (including subscriptions for Whereabouts Pro) are processed entirely by Apple. Your payment information is never seen or stored by Inventist. To manage or cancel a subscription, open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad.

Children

Our apps are appropriate for ages 4+ and contain no third-party advertising, no chat, no social features that share personal information, and no data collection. They are designed to be safe for children to use independently.

This website

inventist.io is a static marketing site. We do not run analytics, set tracking cookies, or fingerprint visitors. The site uses localStorage only to remember a single non-personal preference (your selected logo concept during our identity exploration). Standard server access logs from our hosting provider may include your IP address and user-agent for security and abuse prevention; these logs are kept for a short period and never sold or shared.

Your rights

Because we don't collect personal data, there's no account or profile to access, export, or delete. You always control your in-app data by managing the app on your device.

If you live in California, the EU/UK, or another region with privacy laws, you have rights to access, correct, and delete personal data we hold about you. We hold none — but if you would like written confirmation of that, email privacy@inventist.io.

Changes

If we ever materially change this policy (for example, adding a new app that does collect data), we will update the "Last updated" date and post a notice on this page before the change takes effect.
