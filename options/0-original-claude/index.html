About the studio

Considered software, made for the people who use it.

Inventist is an independent studio focused on a small number of native iOS apps. We care about the texture of an app — the haptic on a button press, the way a screen settles, the feeling that something was made by people who use it every day.

We build for the App Store because we believe the best educational experiences feel less like school and more like a game you keep coming back to. Every app we ship is designed and developed in-house — no white-label, no shortcuts.

Native by default

Built with Swift, SwiftUI, and the platform's own design language — not wrapped, not ported.

Curiosity-led

Geography, history, science — the kinds of subjects that reward a few minutes a day.

Privacy-first

Our apps don't collect personal data. No accounts, no trackers, no ads. Just the experience.
