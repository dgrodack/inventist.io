Whereabouts FAQ

The globe is solid orange — there's no map.

Force-quit the app and re-open it. If the issue persists, restart your device. The map texture is bundled with the app and should always be available offline.

I lost my streak after switching phones.

Whereabouts stores your streak locally on the device, so a fresh install on a new phone starts at zero. We're working on iCloud sync for a future update — email us if you'd like an update when it ships.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple, not by us. Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad and tap Whereabouts to manage or cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.

I purchased Pro but the app still asks me to upgrade.

Open the Whereabouts paywall and tap Restore Purchases. If it still doesn't work, email us with the Apple ID email you used and the approximate purchase date and we'll sort it.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. Whereabouts does not collect, transmit, or share personal data. See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.

Can I play offline?

Yes — the entire game, including the 3D globe and all puzzle data, runs offline. You only need an internet connection for in-app purchases.

Will Whereabouts come to iPad / Mac / Apple Watch?

iPhone first. iPad is on the roadmap. Mac and Watch are under consideration. Tell us where you'd like to play it.
