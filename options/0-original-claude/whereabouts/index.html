Find your way around the world.

Whereabouts is a daily geography puzzle on a beautiful 3D globe. Five rounds, three minutes — drop a pin, score the distance, build a streak. Cities, capitals, countries and landmarks across the planet.

Download on the App Store TestFlight rolling soon

For iPhone · iOS 18+ · Free with optional Pro upgrade

A new puzzle every day

One globe. Endless places to find.

A 10K equirectangular map wrapped on a real RealityKit sphere. Spin it with a finger, pinch to zoom, tap to drop a pin. Confirm your guess and watch the gold answer pin appear with the great-circle distance.

What's in it

Built for the daily habit.

Three minutes a day, designed to feel like a small ritual rather than a game session.

Real 3D globe

Built with SwiftUI and RealityKit. A genuine sphere — drag, pinch and rotate the way you'd expect.

Four modes

Countries, capitals, cities and landmarks. 1,185 places hand-curated for daily puzzles.

Streaks & XP

Daily streaks, badges, and an XP curve that rewards proximity over guesswork.

Themed packs

Cascade peaks, world lighthouses, the Ring of Fire — extra map packs available with Pro.

Haptic feedback

Subtle taps when you drop a pin, confirm a guess, and unlock a badge.

No data collection

Streaks, scores and settings stay on your device. No accounts, no trackers, no ads.

Whereabouts Pro

Optional. Honest pricing. No tricks.
