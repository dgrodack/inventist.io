i. Summary

Inventist LLC ("Inventist", "we", "us") publishes Whereabouts and any future apps listed on this site. Our apps are rated Data Not Collected on the App Store. We do not gather, store, sell, or share personal information about you. There are no accounts to create, no trackers to refuse, no ads to dismiss.

The short version, in plainer English: We didn't build a server that knows you. Your streak lives on your phone. If you delete the app, the data goes with it. If you write to us, we keep that email until we've answered, and then we don't.

ii. What we collect

Nothing about you, personally. Whereabouts has no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking, no advertising identifiers, and no in-app surveys. We do not request your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, or any other personal data to use the app.

The only network requests Whereabouts makes are to Apple's StoreKit, when you choose to subscribe or restore a purchase. Apple handles that transaction under their own privacy terms; we receive a receipt confirming entitlement, but no personal information.

iii. What stays on your device

Whereabouts stores your progress locally on your iPhone using the system's UserDefaults store. This includes:

  • — Your current streak, longest streak, and total XP.
  • — Earned badges and per-mode statistics.
  • — Your purchase entitlement (whether Pro is active).
  • — Settings such as preferred map style and units.

This data never leaves the device. We have no copy of it. Deleting the app from your iPhone deletes everything; offloading the app preserves the data for next time, in line with iOS conventions.

iv. Third parties

Whereabouts does not embed third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, attribution, crash reporting, or social sign-in. The app does not share data with anyone, because there is no data to share.

The only third party we interact with is Apple, for the App Store and StoreKit. Apple's privacy practices govern that interaction.

v. Purchases

In-app purchases (monthly, yearly, lifetime) are processed by Apple via StoreKit. Inventist does not see your payment method, billing address, Apple ID, or any other personal account information. We receive only a cryptographic receipt that confirms whether your Pro entitlement is active.

vi. Children

Whereabouts is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because the app collects nothing, parents and guardians can let curious kids play it with confidence.

vii. Your rights

We can't show you, export, or delete personal data we don't have. If you are a resident of the EU/EEA, the UK, or California, you may still write to us with any questions about your rights under GDPR, UK GDPR, or the CCPA; we will respond within a reasonable time.

viii. Changes

If our practices ever change in a way that affects you, we'll update this page and bump the effective date at the top. If a change ever requires notice, we'll add an in-app notice as well — but as a one-room studio, we don't expect this to happen often.

ix. Contact

Privacy questions go to privacy@inventist.io. General correspondence is welcome at hello@inventist.io. Inventist LLC is a Florida-registered limited liability company; we do not maintain a public mailing address.
