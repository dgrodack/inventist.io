§ 04  House style

"Every app should feel like it was made by someone who would still answer your email. We try to keep that posture even after we've shipped, and especially when no one is watching."

i.  Native, always

Built in Swift, for the device in your hand.

No webviews dressed up as apps. No frameworks the platform didn't ask for. We use SwiftUI, RealityKit, and the system the way Apple intended it.

ii.  Quiet by default

Data not collected. Nothing to leak.

No accounts, no analytics, no third-party trackers, no ads. Your streak lives on the device, where it belongs. The privacy label says it because it's true.

iii.  Made for keeping

A small list, kept current for years.

We'd rather maintain a few favorites than ship a dozen we couldn't love. Updates come because they should, not on a quarter's schedule.
