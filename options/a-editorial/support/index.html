§ 01  Frequently asked

About Whereabouts
The questions that show up first.

How does a daily puzzle work?

+

Every day, Whereabouts hands you the same five rounds as everyone else. Each round shows you a place to find — a country, a capital, a city, or a landmark. Spin the globe, drop a pin where you think the place is, and the app reveals the answer with the distance and a sentence about the place. A round takes about thirty seconds; a full daily, about three minutes.

Do I need an account to play?

+

No. Whereabouts has no accounts at all — no email, no Apple ID prompt, no sign-in screen. Your streak, XP and badges live on your iPhone, in UserDefaults. If you offload the app, your progress is preserved. If you delete it, your progress is deleted with it.

How do I move my streak to a new iPhone?

+

Use Apple's encrypted iPhone-to-iPhone transfer or a full encrypted iCloud backup. Both restore UserDefaults, which is where Whereabouts stores your progress. Because we don't keep a server-side copy of anything, Apple's transfer is the only way to migrate.

What does Pro unlock?

+

The free experience includes the daily puzzle, every day, forever. Pro unlocks the back catalog of dailies (so you can play missed days), the full themed pack library (currently twelve packs and growing), and any new packs that ship while your subscription is active. Lifetime gets all of that, including future packs.

Can I restore a previous purchase?

+

Yes. Open Whereabouts, go to Settings → Pro, and tap "Restore Purchase". This pings StoreKit, which checks the Apple ID associated with your device. If a Pro entitlement is on file, it'll be restored immediately. We don't see the request — Apple does.

How is the leaderboard private if there's a leaderboard?

+

The leaderboard is opt-in. If you choose to participate, you pick a short display name and we generate a one-time anonymous identifier on-device. Friends are added by code, not by phone book or contacts permission. Score submissions are signed and contain no personal information.

What devices does Whereabouts support?

+

Whereabouts is an iPhone app, iOS 18 and later. The 3D globe relies on RealityKit and the modern SwiftUI stack. iPad and Mac versions aren't promised; if they happen, we'll write about it on the studio page first.

I think I found a bug.

+

Please write to hello@inventist.io with your iOS version, your iPhone model, and what you were doing when it happened. Screenshots are very helpful. We read every message and we'll write back, even if the only honest answer is "we don't know yet, but we're looking".

Will you suggest a place that should be in the catalog?

+

Yes, please. Send a short note to hello@inventist.io with the name of the place and one sentence about why it earns its inclusion. We don't promise to add everything, but we read every suggestion and curate them into the next pack.

Refunds?

+

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple. Refund requests are handled through reportaproblem.apple.com; Apple decides on a case-by-case basis. We can't issue refunds on Apple's behalf. If you write to us about it, we'll point you in the right direction and back you up if you need it.
