How we work

Three things we
care about, deeply.

01

Native or nothing.

SwiftUI, RealityKit, the system gestures. We build for iPhone the way iPhone wants to be built — fast, fluent, and at home on your device.

02

Privacy by default.

No accounts. No trackers. No analytics SDKs. Your progress lives on your phone in UserDefaults — exactly where it should.

03

Slow and proper.

We'd rather ship one app a year that you keep on your home screen than ship twelve that get deleted. Craft over churn, every time.
