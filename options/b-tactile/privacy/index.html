Privacy: Data Not Collected

Apple's privacy nutrition label for every Inventist app is empty. No data is linked to you. No data is used to track you. Nothing leaves your device.

1. Who we are

Inventist LLC ("Inventist," "we," "us") is an independent iOS studio organized in the State of Florida, United States. We make native iPhone apps, including Whereabouts. This privacy policy applies to every app we publish and to the website at inventist.io.

2. What we collect

We do not collect personal information. The privacy nutrition labels on our App Store listings reflect this — every column is "Data Not Collected."

That means specifically:

  • We do not require an account.
  • We do not have a user database.
  • We do not embed analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Sentry, no Bugsnag).
  • We do not embed advertising or attribution SDKs.
  • We do not access your Contacts, Photos, Health, Microphone, or Location unless an app explicitly asks you for permission for a specific feature — and right now, none of our apps do.
  • We do not read or transmit data from your iCloud account.

3. How the apps work

Your progress in Whereabouts — your streak, XP, badges, completed map packs, scoring history — is stored on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults storage. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export or share it.

The 3D globe assets, place data, and themed map packs are bundled inside the app or downloaded once via Apple's standard delivery. We do not log what you tap on, how long you play, or which guesses you made.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

If you buy Whereabouts Pro (monthly, yearly, or lifetime) or any other in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never see your name, billing address, or payment details. Apple shares with us only the anonymous information needed to validate that the purchase happened.

Refund requests are handled by Apple under their App Store policies. You can request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

5. Third parties

The only third parties involved in delivering our apps to you are Apple (as the platform vendor and payment processor) and standard Apple network infrastructure (App Store delivery, push notification gateway if you enable notifications). We do not share data with marketing partners, ad networks, data brokers, or analytics providers — because we do not have any data to share.

This website (inventist.io) is served as static files. We do not run server-side analytics, set marketing cookies, or embed third-party trackers.

6. Children

Our apps are appropriate for general audiences and are rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we do not collect data at all, this is straightforward.

7. Your rights

Various jurisdictions (including the EU's GDPR, the UK's UK GDPR, California's CCPA/CPRA, and others) grant individuals rights regarding their personal data — to access it, correct it, delete it, or object to its processing.

Because we do not collect personal data, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. If you would like to exercise any data right, or simply confirm in writing that we hold no personal data about you, write to privacy@inventist.io and we will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how our apps handle data — for example, if a future feature requires opt-in access to a sensor — we will update this policy and surface the change clearly inside the affected app before the new version ships. We will not retroactively start collecting information about activity from prior versions.
