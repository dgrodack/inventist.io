Frequently asked

If your question isn't here, email and ask. We'll add it.

What is Whereabouts?

Whereabouts is a daily geography puzzle for iPhone. Each day there's one new puzzle made of five rounds: a place name appears, you spin a 3D globe, and tap to drop a pin where you think the place is. The closer your pin to the actual location, the more XP you earn. Five rounds takes about three minutes.

What devices does Whereabouts run on?

Whereabouts is built for iPhone running iOS 18 or later. It uses SwiftUI for the interface and RealityKit to render the 3D globe natively. There is no iPad layout (yet) and no Mac, web, or Android version planned at this time.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no sign-up, no login, no password, no email needed. Your streak, scores, and badges are stored on your device. If you delete the app, that data goes with it — it isn't backed up to a cloud service we control.

What's in Pro, and what's free?

The daily puzzle — five rounds across all four modes (Countries, Capitals, Cities, Landmarks) — is free for everyone, every day, forever.

Pro unlocks every themed map pack (currently 12+), the weekly leaderboard for friendly competition, and Pro-only badges. Pricing: $2.99/month (first week free), $19.99/year (first week free, ~44% off the monthly rate), or $49.99 one-time lifetime.

What data does the app collect about me?

None. The Apple App Store privacy nutrition label for Whereabouts is "Data Not Collected" across the board. We don't run analytics, we don't have user accounts, we don't transmit your scores. Everything stays on your device. The full privacy policy is here.

How does the leaderboard work without accounts?

The Pro leaderboard uses Apple's Game Center, which handles the identity layer with your existing Apple ID. We don't see your real name or email — Game Center surfaces only your nickname and your weekly score totals. You can opt out anytime in Settings.

I missed a day. Did I lose my streak?

Yes — but it's just a number. The app won't pester you about it. You can start a new streak today. We're working on a once-a-month "freeze" token for Pro players who travel.

Will more map packs be added?

Yes — roughly one new pack per month, included in Pro. Recent and upcoming packs include Cascade Peaks, Ring of Fire (volcanoes), World Lighthouses, Capitals at Latitude, and a few seasonal surprises.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email hello@inventist.io with a description of what happened, what you expected, and your iPhone model + iOS version if you happen to know them. A screenshot helps a lot. We do read every report.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by us. To cancel: open the App Store on your iPhone, tap your profile icon, tap "Subscriptions," select Whereabouts Pro, then "Cancel Subscription." If you bought lifetime, there's nothing to cancel — you're set.

Can I get a refund?

Yes — Apple handles all refunds for App Store purchases. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find Whereabouts in your purchase history, and request a refund. Apple usually responds within 48 hours.

Is Whereabouts kid-friendly?

Yes. The App Store rating is 4+. Place names and landmarks are factual, with no political commentary or violent imagery. There are no ads, no in-app messages from strangers, and no chat features.

Where can I suggest a feature or a place?

Email hello@inventist.io and tell us. Specifically helpful: a missing capital you'd like to see, a themed pack idea (with a few example places), or a small UX paper-cut. We keep a public-ish road note and your idea may end up there.
