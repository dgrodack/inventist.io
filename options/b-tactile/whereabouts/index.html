The daily ritual

Five rounds. Three minutes.

A pleasant little routine for your morning coffee or your subway ride. Then you go on with your day.

1

Open the app

Today's puzzle is waiting on the home screen. No login, no nag.

2

Read the prompt

"Find Reykjavík." That's it. The whole world is your search box.

3

Spin the globe

Drag, pinch, tilt. It's a real 3D sphere — wrapped in a 10K equirectangular Earth.

4

Drop your pin

Tap to commit. See how far off you were and how many points you earned.

5

Come back tomorrow

Your streak grows. Your map of the world quietly fills in.

A real planet

Not a flat map.
A globe that spins.

Built with SwiftUI and RealityKit, the globe is an actual 3D sphere — not a textured plane pretending. A 10,240-pixel equirectangular Earth wraps around it, so when you zoom in close, the coastlines hold up.

  • Inertia-driven spin with realistic damping
  • Pinch-to-zoom from continent to coastline
  • Built natively on Apple's RealityKit pipeline
  • Smooth on every device that runs iOS 18
Honest scoring

You'll always know how close.

Drop a pin and see your guess line up against the truth, distance measured in real kilometers. Score scales with accuracy — a near-miss in a busy region still feels rewarding.

  • Great-circle distance, calculated honestly
  • XP curve tuned to feel generous, not stingy
  • One-tap shareable summary card
  • Every guess marked on your personal map
Habit, kindly

Streaks that don't bully you.

Daily play earns XP, badges, and a flame next to your streak. Miss a day? You won't get a guilt-trip notification. We trust you'll come back when you want to.

  • One puzzle a day — no infinite scroll
  • Earn badges for travel themes, modes, milestones
  • Optional weekly leaderboard among Pro players
  • Notifications are off by default
