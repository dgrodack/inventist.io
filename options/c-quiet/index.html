What we believe

PRINCIPLES — 03
01 / Native

iOS-first, on purpose.

Every app we make is written in Swift, designed for iPhone, and tuned to one platform's strengths. No web wrappers. No cross-platform compromise.

02 / Quiet

Earn the user's attention.

No accounts to make. No notifications begging you back. No trackers. The app is the product, and the product is the whole relationship.

03 / Curious

Learning that feels like play.

Geography today. Other corners of the world tomorrow. We pick subjects worth studying for their own sake, then make them daily and small.
