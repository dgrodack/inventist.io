Frequently asked

FAQ — 12 ENTRIES
01 How does the daily puzzle reset?
A new puzzle drops at midnight in your local time zone. You have until the next midnight to play it — five rounds, one chance each. If you miss a day, your streak resets, but everything else is preserved.
02 Why is there no account?
Because there is nothing for an account to hold. Whereabouts stores your progress on your iPhone. No server, no sign-in, no password to remember, no email to leak. The trade-off is that progress does not currently sync across devices — see the next question.
03 Can I sync progress between my iPhone and iPad?
Whereabouts is iPhone-only at launch. iCloud sync between an iPhone and an iPad is on the roadmap, and would happen entirely through Apple's CloudKit (so your data would still never touch our servers).
04 I lost my streak — can you restore it?
Honestly, no, and we are sorry. Because we do not collect data, we have no record of your streak — only your iPhone does. If iOS deletes the app's data (uninstall, restore from backup without the app, etc.) the streak is genuinely gone. The good news: starting again is part of the ritual.
05 How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Settings → Apple ID (your name at the top) → Subscriptions → Whereabouts → Cancel. Your Pro features remain active until the end of the current billing period.
06 What does Pro actually unlock?
All themed map packs, unlimited daily history, deeper stats, and any future Pro-only modes. The free version is the full daily puzzle and the four core modes — Pro is the deeper shelf for people who want more.
07 Is there an Android version?
No, and there are no plans for one. Whereabouts is built in SwiftUI and RealityKit specifically for the iPhone. Doing it well on one platform is the whole point.
08 My device feels warm or the battery drains during play.
The 3D globe is real-time rendered on your device, so on older iPhones a daily round can be more demanding than a typical app. We continue to tune performance with each release. If a device feels uncomfortably warm, please let us know which model you are on — we read every report.
09 A place is mislabelled or misplaced.
It happens — we hand-curated 1 185 places and we will get some wrong. Email us with the place and round number and we will check it before the next release.
10 Can I suggest a themed map pack?
Yes, please. Many of our packs began as a single email. Tell us the theme and a few sample places and we will see if we can build it.
11 Press, podcasts, or partnership inquiries?
Write to hello@inventist.io with the words "press", "podcast" or "partnership" in the subject. We do read these.
12 I just want to say thank you.
That works too. hello@inventist.io. Truly, it makes the rest of the work easier.
