Privacy
Data not collected.

Inventist apps do not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit your personal data. There are no accounts. There are no third-party trackers. There are no advertising SDKs. This document exists to say so plainly, and to satisfy Apple's App Store requirements.

01 The summary in one sentence

We do not collect data. Everything Whereabouts knows about you — your streak, your XP, your badges, your map pack progress, your daily history — is stored on your iPhone in UserDefaults. We never see any of it.

02 Scope

This policy covers all software published by Inventist LLC, a Florida limited liability company, including but not limited to the Whereabouts iPhone app and any future apps shipped under the Inventist name. It also covers the marketing site at inventist.io.

03 App Store nutrition label

For every Inventist app, our App Store privacy disclosure reads, end to end:

Data used to track youNone
Data linked to youNone
Data not linked to youNone
Third-party SDKsNone
Account requiredNo
Network usageRequired only for App Store purchases

04 What we do not do

  • We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address.
  • We do not embed analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Segment, no anything).
  • We do not embed advertising SDKs and we do not show ads.
  • We do not embed crash reporting SDKs of any kind.
  • We do not collect your Advertising Identifier (IDFA) or any device identifier.
  • We do not collect your location, your contacts, your photos, your microphone, or any other system permission. The app never asks.
  • We do not run our own servers. There is no Inventist backend that holds user data because there is no Inventist backend.

05 What stays on your iPhone

Whereabouts stores everything it needs to function locally on your device, using Apple's UserDefaults system:

  • Your current and longest streak.
  • Your XP, level, and earned badges.
  • Your daily history and per-round results.
  • Which themed map packs you have opened or completed.
  • Your settings (sound, haptics, accent preferences).

If you delete the app, all of this is removed by iOS. We have no copy.

06 Third parties

The only third party involved in any Inventist app is Apple, by virtue of distribution through the App Store and processing of in-app purchases through StoreKit. Apple's handling of that data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

07 Purchases & subscriptions

Whereabouts offers an optional Pro subscription and a one-time lifetime purchase. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple. Inventist receives anonymised payout data from Apple — totals and territories — but never receives your name, email, payment method, or any data that could identify you personally.

08 Children

Whereabouts is suitable for all ages and contains no advertising, account creation, or external links. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

09 Your rights

Because we do not collect data, requests under GDPR, CCPA, or similar regulations have an unusually short answer: there is nothing on our side to access, export, correct, or delete. The data is on your device. You control it directly through iOS Settings and by uninstalling the app.

You are nevertheless welcome to contact us at privacy@inventist.io with any privacy question. We answer every email.

10 Changes to this policy

We will update this page if our practices ever change — for example, if we add a feature that requires a network call. The effective date at the top will move accordingly, and material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
