Frequently asked

What is Whereabouts?

Whereabouts is our flagship iOS app: a fast, daily geography puzzle on a real 3D globe. Five rounds. One world. Each round shows you a place — Rome, Marseille, Lima — and you drop a pin where you think it is. The closer you are, the more points you score. A daily run takes about three minutes.

What devices does Whereabouts support?

Whereabouts requires iOS 18 or later on any iPhone. iPad and Mac (Apple Silicon) support runs the same iOS build in compatibility mode.

Is there a free version?

Yes. The daily round and the Countries pack are free, forever, no account required. Whereabouts Pro unlocks all of the themed packs — including World Capitals, World Landmarks, Bible Lands, WW2 History, and Sports Stadiums — plus future packs as they're added.

How much does Pro cost?

$2.99 / month, $19.99 / year, or $49.99 for a lifetime unlock. All purchases go through Apple's StoreKit. Family Sharing is supported.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open Whereabouts → Settings → Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase, it will be restored instantly.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by us. Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Whereabouts → Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for Whereabouts. We don't have access to your billing.

Do you collect my data?

No. Inventist apps are Data Not Collected on the App Store nutrition label. Your progress, streaks, and settings stay on your device. See our privacy policy for the full breakdown.

Is Whereabouts kid-safe?

Yes — rated 4+. There are no ads, no chat, no profiles, no sign-in, and nothing to spend money on inside the app beyond the optional Pro unlock (which a parent or guardian must approve via Apple's standard purchase flow).

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email contact@inventist.io with your iOS version, your iPhone model, and a quick description of what happened. Screenshots help. We read every message.

I have an idea for a new feature or a new map pack.

We love this. Send it to contact@inventist.io. We can't promise everything will ship, but every suggestion is read and considered.

I'm a journalist or App Store reviewer — how do I verify the company?

Inventist LLC is a registered Florida limited liability company. Reach contact@inventist.io from a verifiable address and we'll respond promptly with whatever you need.
