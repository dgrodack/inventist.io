About Inventist

A small Florida company
making careful apps.

Inventist LLC is a Florida-registered limited liability company. We make iOS apps slowly and ship them only when they're worth your time. We don't track you. We don't sell ads. We don't beg for your attention.

Whereabouts is our first app. More are on the way — all native to iOS, all designed for the same audiences: parents who want something safe to hand to a curious kid, homeschoolers who want real subject matter, and teachers who want a quiet five-minute classroom activity that actually teaches something.
